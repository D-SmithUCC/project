In today's world, it can seem impossible to affect meaningful change upon the issues that matter most. And while this website will not be the solution to those problems, the sites below have been created by people who are trying their very best to solve a variety of issues we have today with education.

The education trust is a forward looking organization that works througout to the world to bring effective education to disadvantaged children. They were established in 1996, and have helped thousads of children since.

Khan academy is an online learning platform leveling the playing field of eductaion; here, everyone has access to informative and well made lessons created by professionals. Khan has been around since 2008 and helps hundres of thousands of learners every year.

World Vision is a not for profit that sponsors underpriveleged youth who have recieved poor or no education. They tackle issues at the core of multi-generational poverty like hunger, lack of water, and poor education.

Thank you so much for donating to these organizations. Know that your sacrifice and genorosity could make all the difference in someone's life, and that by donating to the above organizations you are making positive change in the world.
